Help & Support

Find answers to common questions about using the Retrigency Hospital System.

Getting Started

What is Retrigency Hospital System?

Retrigency is a hospital management system designed to help staff manage patient records, track admissions, and monitor hospital activity. It includes features for registering patients, searching records, viewing patient details, and generating reports.

How do I add a new patient?

Click the "Add New Patient" card on the Dashboard or the "Add Patient" button on the Patients page. Fill in the required fields (first name, surname, and date of birth). You can also add optional details such as other names, address, gender, contact, blood type, Ghana card number, emergency contacts, and a patient photo. Age is calculated automatically from the date of birth. Click "Add Patient" and confirm to save the record.

What format should the Ghana card number be in?

The Ghana card number must start with "GHA-" (for example, GHA-123456789-1). The system will show an error if the number does not begin with this prefix.

Searching & Managing Patients

How do I search for a patient?

On the Patients page, use the search bar and choose a search type from the dropdown. You can search by "All fields" (name, Patient ID, or Ghana card), or limit the search to "Name", "Patient ID", or "Ghana Card" only. Type your search term and the results update automatically.

How do I view a patient's full details?

Click on any patient row in the Patients table. This opens the Patient Details page, where you can see all recorded information including contact details, Ghana card, emergency contacts, and photo.

How do I discharge a patient?

Open the patient's detail page by clicking their row, then click the "Discharge Patient" button. The patient's status will change to "Discharged" and the update will appear across the system.

How do I delete a patient record?

You can delete a patient from the Patient Details page by clicking "Delete Patient" and confirming in the popup, or by clicking the red trash icon in the Actions column on the Patients table. You will be asked to confirm before the record is permanently removed.

Dashboard & Statistics

What do the dashboard statistics mean?

Total Patients: The total number of patients in the system. Today's Admissions: Patients admitted today. Face ID Scans: Number of face identification scans performed. Active Cases: Patients currently in hospital with "Active" status. These statistics update automatically as you add, discharge, or delete patients.

What is the Recent Activity section?

The Recent Activity section shows recent actions such as new patient registrations, patient discharges, and record deletions, along with when they occurred (e.g., "5 minutes ago", "1 hour ago").

Face ID & Security

What is Face ID used for?

Face ID allows staff to scan a patient's face for identification purposes. This feature helps verify patient identity and can be linked to patient records.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" to open the password retrieval form. Enter your Staff ID, email, and phone number, then choose whether to receive the reset link via SMS or email.
